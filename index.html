Project Overview

This transformative project enhances digital education in Bhutan by implementing comprehensive e-Learning systems and building ICT capacity in selected schools across the kingdom.

Our initiative focuses on developing ICT skills among teachers, students, and administrators while establishing robust campus networks and connecting schools to DrukREN to support online learning, resource sharing, and educational accessibility nationwide.

Key Outcomes

🌐 Schools Connected to DrukREN

Successfully connected three schools to Bhutan's high-speed national research and education network, enabling reliable e-Learning access and digital resource sharing.

💻 ICT Infrastructure Deployed

Installed comprehensive LAN infrastructure, configured computer labs, and deployed essential ICT equipment across all participating schools.

🖥️ Centralized Learning System

Deployed and configured centralized servers and Learning Management Systems to support effective teaching and learning across the network.

📚 Training Content Developed

Created comprehensive content for system administration, e-Learning content development, and user training to ensure sustainable implementation.

👥 Capacity Building Achievement

Trained over 150 teachers, students, and ICT staff in e-Learning platform usage, content development, and network management skills.

🎯 National Outreach Success

Conducted successful national webinar promoting e-Learning practices and encouraging widespread participation across Bhutan's education sector.

🏆 Official Project Completion

Officially inaugurated e-Learning systems and successfully handed over fully functional digital education infrastructure to participating schools.

🔄 Sustainability Framework

Established systems for ongoing technical support, platform usage tracking, and alignment with national digital education initiatives.
